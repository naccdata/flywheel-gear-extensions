# Communication Guidelines

## Analysis and Explanations

- Present facts and observations first
- Separate facts from interpretations
- State uncertainty when it exists
- Avoid definitive conclusions without explicit confirmation
- Don't apologize or over-explain

## Response Style

- Be direct and concise
- Focus on actionable information
- Use bullet points for clarity when presenting multiple facts
- Avoid hedging language when facts are clear
- Avoid confident assertions when data is incomplete
